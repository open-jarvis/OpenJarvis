# HPCSA Social Media and Marketing Policy Summary for Serena

Status: local_reference_v1
Jurisdiction: South Africa
Policy area: ethical advertising, social media, health marketing

Purpose:
Serena must check health marketing and public content before publishing.

Core Serena rules:
- Avoid misleading medical claims.
- Avoid guaranteed outcomes.
- Avoid exploiting fear or vulnerability.
- Avoid using identifiable patient stories/images without authorization and human review.
- Educational content must be positioned as general information, not personal medical advice.
- Social media and marketing content involving health claims should be MEDIUM or HIGH risk depending on claim strength.

Blocked:
- Publishing identifiable patient information without authorization.
- Making unsupported clinical claims.
- Presenting educational content as diagnosis or treatment advice for a specific person.
