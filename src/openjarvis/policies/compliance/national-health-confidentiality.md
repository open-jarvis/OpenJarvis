# National Health Confidentiality Policy Summary for Serena

Status: local_reference_v1
Jurisdiction: South Africa
Policy area: health confidentiality

Purpose:
Serena must treat patient health information as confidential.

Core Serena rules:
- Health status, treatment information, and clinical records are confidential.
- Do not disclose identifiable patient health information without appropriate authorization.
- Do not use patient information for marketing or public content unless explicit consent/authorization is confirmed and human review occurs.
- Patient/health data should be labelled and handled as HIGH risk.

Blocked:
- Publishing identifiable patient health data without authorization.
- Sharing patient records externally without reporting and approval.
- Using OCR/camera/screen capture to silently collect patient information.
