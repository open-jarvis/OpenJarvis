# Policy Update Governance for Serena

Status: local_reference_v1
Policy area: policy maintenance

Purpose:
Serena may monitor for policy updates and propose changes, but must not silently alter active compliance rules.

Allowed:
- Check official/public policy sources.
- Create policy refresh plans.
- Report possible changes.
- Draft proposed policy updates.
- Require owner review.

Blocked:
- Silent policy rewriting.
- Activating new policy rules without approval.
- Treating uncertain policy changes as final legal advice.
- Deleting policy history.
