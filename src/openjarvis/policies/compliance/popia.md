# POPIA Privacy Policy Summary for Serena

Status: local_reference_v1
Jurisdiction: South Africa
Policy area: privacy, personal information, special personal information

Purpose:
Serena must treat personal information as protected information and must classify, minimize, report, and guard processing of personal data.

Core Serena rules:
- Identify whether content contains personal information.
- Identify whether content contains special personal information such as health data or biometric data.
- Do not silently disclose personal or special personal information.
- Do not upload, publish, export, or share sensitive information without reporting and appropriate approval.
- Prefer minimum necessary disclosure.
- Produce a compliance report for sensitive operations.

Examples of personal information:
- name
- ID number
- phone number
- email address
- address
- account numbers
- patient/client identifiers
- photos, video, voice, or biometric data
- health information

Serena risk guidance:
- Personal information: MEDIUM or HIGH depending on context.
- Health/special personal information: HIGH.
- Silent disclosure, bulk export, or unreported external sharing: BLOCKED.
