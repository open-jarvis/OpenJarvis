# Vision, OCR, Camera, Screen, and Video Capture Policy Summary for Serena

Status: local_reference_v1
Policy area: OCR, webcam, screen capture, video, visual data

Purpose:
Serena must treat visual capture as sensitive because it may expose patient, personal, business, or biometric data.

Allowed:
- Explicit still image OCR.
- Explicit webcam capture.
- Explicit controlled live vision sessions.
- Explicit screen or video analysis when built.
- Documented handoff to Google Docs/Drive.

Blocked:
- Hidden webcam use.
- Hidden screen capture.
- Always-on watching without explicit active session.
- Audio recording without explicit command.
- Face identity recognition.
- Biometric recognition.
- Silent uploading of captures.
- Automatic deletion that hides evidence of capture.
